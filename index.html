About us

The London Knights Cricket Club was founded in 2021 by a group of friends look to play together and play good competitive cricket. In the first 10 games of red ball cricket the team won 10 consecutive games and decided it was time to make a bid for Saturday league cricket. The team have now been entered in the Kent Cricket League looking for premiership glory in there first year in the league. London Knights Cricket Club provides a friendly multi-cultural environment to play sport and make life long friends. Enquire for more information!
